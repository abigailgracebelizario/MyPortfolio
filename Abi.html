Hello! My name is Abi!

I enjoy writing my own books. Right now I am working on a book called Glenlake Academy.

Glenlake Academy is about a group of friends who are in eighth grade that go to boarding school.

Andre finds himself in a lot of trouble with the principal of the school because he's dating her nephew, which she doesn't approve of at all.

Relationships are in hot water and the tension between them is building up.

His death rattles his friends and the entire school. Can his friends or the police find out who the killer is before they get away with murder?
